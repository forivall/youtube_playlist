Youtube Playlist Bookmarklet

about

Hi! This is a bookmarklet that scans a page for all youtube links, and plays them back-to-back.

installing

Simply drag this bookmark to your bookmarks bar: Play Videos

Here's the page I built it for, but that might not be your cup of tea, so here's a page with videos of cute animals.
